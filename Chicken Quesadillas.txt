Title: Chicken Quesadillas
Pub date: December 1, 2011
Category: Recipes, 

Adapted from all recipes "Pico de Gallo Chicken Quesadillas"

Pico de Gallo

2 tomatoes, diced
1/2 onion, finely chopped
2 limes, juiced
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
salt and pepper to taste

--

Shredded Chicken (2 breasts -use 1/2 of pico)
1/2 onion, thinly sliced
1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
1 yellow pepper, thinly sliced
1/2 can of corn
Flour tortillas
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Toppings:
Sour Cream
Salsa
Avocado

1. In a small bowl, combine tomatoes, onion, lime juice, cilantro, jalapeno, salt and pepper.

2. Mix pico with shredded chicken and set aside, or refrigerate if making ahead.

3. Char peppers, onions and corn in a very hot cast iron skillet.  Can do each in a separate batch.

4. In a heavy skillet, heat one flour tortilla. Spread 1/4 cup shredded cheese on the tortilla and top with the chicken mixture and roasted vegetables. Sprinkle another layer of cheese over the chicken and top with another tortilla. When bottom tortilla is lightly brown and cheese has started to melt, flip quesadilla and cook on the opposite side. Remove quesadilla from skillet and cut into quarters. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
