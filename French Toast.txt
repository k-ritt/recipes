# French Toast
###### October 24, 2011
###### Bread, Recipes, 

http://allrecipes.com/recipe/french-toast-i/detail.aspx
Servings: 3
"A basic French toast recipe that works with many types of bread - white, whole wheat, cinnamon-raisin, Italian or French. Serve hot with butter or margarine and maple syrup."
INGREDIENTS:
<table width="100%" border="0" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0">
<tbody>
<tr>
<td valign="top" width="50%">
6 thick slices bread
2 eggs
2/3 cup milk
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)</td>
<td valign="top" width="50%">
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
salt to taste</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
DIRECTIONS:
<table border="0" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0">
<tbody>
<tr>
<td valign="top">1.</td>
<td valign="top">Beat together egg, milk, salt, desired spices and vanilla.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign="top">2.</td>
<td valign="top">Heat a lightly oiled griddle of skillet over medium-high flame.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign="top">3.</td>
<td valign="top">Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides. Place in pan, and cook on both sides until golden. Serve hot.</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
