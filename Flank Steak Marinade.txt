Title: Flank Steak Marinade
Pub date: October 22, 2012
Category: Beef, Recipes, 

<div>1/2 cup soy sauce</div>
<div>2 tablespoons brown sugar</div>
<div>2 tablespoons lemon juice</div>
<div>2 tablespoons olive oil</div>
<div>2 cloves garlic, minced</div>
<div>1 tablespoon minced onion</div>
<div>1 teaspoon ground ginger</div>
<div>1/2 teaspoon black pepper</div>
<div>1 (1 1/2-pound) beef flank steak</div>
<div>
<ol>
	<li>In a shallow bowl, combine soy sauce, brown sugar, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, onion, ginger and pepper.</li>
	<li>Coat steak with marinade, cover, and refrigerate for at least 6 hours.</li>
	<li>Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil grate</li>
	<li>Grill steak for 7 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness (130-135 for medium rare).</li>
</ol>
