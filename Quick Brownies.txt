Title: Quick Brownies
Pub date: February 21, 2014
Category: Dessert, Recipes, 

<span itemprop="ingredient" itemscope="" itemtype="http://data-vocabulary.org/RecipeIngredient"><span itemprop="amount">1 cup</span> <span itemprop="name">Butter</span></span>
<span itemprop="ingredient" itemscope="" itemtype="http://data-vocabulary.org/RecipeIngredient"><span itemprop="amount">2 cups</span> <span itemprop="name">White Sugar</span></span>
<span itemprop="ingredient" itemscope="" itemtype="http://data-vocabulary.org/RecipeIngredient"><span itemprop="amount">4 whole</span> <span itemprop="name">Eggs</span></span>
<span itemprop="ingredient" itemscope="" itemtype="http://data-vocabulary.org/RecipeIngredient"><span itemprop="amount">2 teaspoons</span> <span itemprop="name">Vanilla Extract</span></span>
<span itemprop="ingredient" itemscope="" itemtype="http://data-vocabulary.org/RecipeIngredient"><span itemprop="amount">⅔ cups</span> <span itemprop="name">Cocoa Powder</span></span>
<span itemprop="ingredient" itemscope="" itemtype="http://data-vocabulary.org/RecipeIngredient"><span itemprop="amount">1 cup</span> <span itemprop="name">Flour</span></span>
<span itemprop="ingredient" itemscope="" itemtype="http://data-vocabulary.org/RecipeIngredient"><span itemprop="amount">½ teaspoons</span> <span itemprop="name">Salt</span></span>
<span itemprop="ingredient" itemscope="" itemtype="http://data-vocabulary.org/RecipeIngredient"><span itemprop="amount">½ teaspoons</span> <span itemprop="name">Baking Powder</span></span>

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9×13 pan.
Melt butter. Remove from heat and add ingredients in the order listed, stirring well by hand.
Bake for 25-30 minutes (My oven took at least 30). Do not overbake.
Cut with a plastic knife (best brownie trick).
It’d also be great to make these using black cocoa.
